# Licensing

## Repository

InheritBench is licensed under Apache-2.0 in `LICENSE`.

Copyright and attribution information is recorded in `NOTICE`. Machine-readable citation metadata
is recorded in `CITATION.cff`.

## Project-Authored Data

OpsRoute is synthetic, project-authored capability data. It defines fictional customer-service
scenarios, policies, tools, reason codes, and safety boundaries; it is not represented as customer
data or a licensed third-party benchmark. The repository distributes the committed OpsRoute source,
schemas, prompts, expected contracts, and generated projections under the repository Apache-2.0
license.

The Purchase Approval capability pack is also project-authored synthetic fixture data. It exists to
test task-neutral loading and evaluation and is not a real model-transfer result.

## Generated Model Outputs

Frozen teacher outputs and final prediction records are generated artifacts, not human-authored
ground truth or fresh browser inference. Their manifests bind them to the exact upstream model,
pinned revision, adapter lineage, prompt, and input records used during generation.

The repository makes these committed artifacts available as part of InheritBench to the extent
covered by the project license. Users remain responsible for reviewing the upstream model cards,
licenses, and any restrictions that apply to their own use or redistribution. This document does
not replace upstream terms or provide a legal conclusion about every downstream use.

InheritBench does not redistribute Qwen, OLMo, or SmolLM model weights. Users obtain those weights
